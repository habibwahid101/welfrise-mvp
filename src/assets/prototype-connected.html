Frequently Asked Questions
How does the 10-to-1 rotation work?
Each level has its own FIFO queue and a separate 0/10 payout-progress counter. On an empty level, the first approved slot becomes the queue head and does not count toward its own cycle. Ten later approved slots trigger one payout to the earliest waiting slot. The first payout occurs at total slot 11, then 21, 31, and so on.
What are the level payout amounts?
Published level payout amounts are Level 1: $20, Level 2: $100, Level 3: $1,000, Level 4: $10,000, and Level 5: $100,000. Payout timing depends on later approved participation and is not guaranteed. A valid referrer earns $1 per approved $10 slot.
When can I withdraw my earnings?
You can withdraw once you have a minimum of $10 in your wallet. Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours after admin approval.
Is KYC required?
KYC (ID verification) is required before you can make withdrawals. You can donate and participate immediately after registration, but KYC must be completed to cash out.
Can I join multiple positions?
Yes. A user may hold up to 10 active waiting slots per level. Packages contain 1, 2, 5, or 10 slots, and a package is blocked if it would exceed the level-specific cap. After completing Level 5, the user may immediately begin a new championship cycle with a new paid Level 1 entry; there is no cooldown and no free re-entry.
